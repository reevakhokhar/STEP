Welcome To Specialized Technological Education Platform

STEP Ed. is a global education platform that works to aid the special needs students all around the world. STEP Ed. strives to create more inclusive classrooms for all the special needs students, even from the comfort of their own homes. Through STEP we hope to simplify schooling that is easy to navigate and help all the special needs students get the education they deserve. Aditionally, we hope to strive to cater to the needs of all types of special need students to make learning for them easier and cooler.

What exactly is STEP Ed?

STEP Ed or The Specialized Technological Education Platform is a platform you can use to study. STEP Ed. hopes to simplify schooling that is easy to navigate and help all the special needs students get the education they deserve. Aditionally, STEP Ed hopes to make remote learning more fun. Sitting in front of a computer screen and listening to lecture is definitely not the best way to learn, so that's why STEP Ed allows the children to have fun and learn at the same time. STEP Ed really hopes to strive to cater to the needs of all types of special need students to make learning for them easier and cooler.

How To Get Started

It's easy, simply create an account! Create an account on STEP Ed and start using it's cool features. You can organize your notes on the dashboard and keep them neat and tidy all in one place. You can also review using the Note-taker, you can speak things and take notes and you can even have the computer read you the notes.

STEP Ed. Features

There are several things your can do on STEP Ed.

Notes Dashboard Use the note dasboard to organize/keep your notes
Note-Taker Use the speech to text note-taking web app to take better notes.
Courses Take courses about school subjects for better understanding.
Articles Read articles that will give you some tips and hacks for school work.
Listen to Notes Use the Note-taking web app to listen to your notes for review.
Make your life Easier Just use Step Ed. and make school work much easier.
